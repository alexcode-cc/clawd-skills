# Freelance Platforms Reference

## Upwork

### Connects
- 2-16 per proposal depending on job tier
- Free: 10/month, Plus ($14.99): 80/month
- Boosted proposals: auction for top 3-4 positions

### Job Scoring (60-Second Triage)
Score 0-5 on: Fit, Scope, Value, Client
- P1 (17-20): Bid now, consider boost
- P2 (14-16): Bid today
- P3 (≤13): Skip, conserve Connects

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Reply rate | >20% |
| Win rate | >10% |
| JSS (Job Success Score) | >90% |

### Profile Must-Haves
- Professional headshot (30% higher response)
- Keyword-rich title with niche
- 300-500 word summary: what you do, who you help, results with numbers
- 4-6 portfolio pieces with context

## Fiverr

### Gig SEO Factors
1. Keyword in title, description, tags, URL slug
2. Click-through rate (thumbnail quality)
3. Conversion rate
4. Response time (<1 hour ideal)

### Level System
| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Level 1 | 60 days + 10 orders + $400 + 4.7 rating |
| Level 2 | 120 days + 50 orders + $2000 + 4.7 rating |
| Top Rated | 180 days + $20K + invitation |

### 3-Tier Pricing
- Basic: Entry point (minimal deliverable)
- Standard: Your real price (full service)
- Premium: White-glove (1.5-2.5× Standard)

**Critical:** URL slug cannot change after first save.

## Freelancer.com

### Bid Allowances
- Free: 8/month
- Plus ($4.95): 50/month
- Professional ($24.95): 100/month

### Contests vs Projects
- Projects: Traditional bidding, milestones
- Contests: Spec work, only enter guaranteed/sealed

## Toptal

### Screening (3% acceptance)
5 stages: Language → Skills Test → Live Screening → Test Project → Ongoing

### Economics
- No bidding — clients matched to you
- Set your own rate ($100-250+/hr typical)
- Premium clients only

---

## Platform-Specific Automation Rules

**All platforms prohibit:**
- Auto-submitting proposals at scale
- Mass messaging / auto-responses
- Account sharing or impersonation
- Scraping data
- Bypassing rate limits or captchas

**Allowed:** AI-assisted drafting with human review and manual send.
