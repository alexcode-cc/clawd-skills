## Restaurant Types by Goal

### Best Value (Under €10)
- **Prato do dia** (daily special) at any tasquinha
- **Cervejaria Madeirense** (Funchal)
- **Tasca Beira Mar** (Câmara de Lobos)
- **Frango no espeto** (rotisserie chicken) anywhere

### Special Occasion
- **Il Gallo d'Oro** — 2 Michelin stars, €€€€
- **William** at Reid's — classic luxury
- **Armazém do Sal** — upscale without pretension

### Best Fish
- **Vila do Peixe** (Câmara de Lobos)
- **Coral** (Câmara de Lobos)
- **Gavião Novo** (Funchal Old Town)

### Best Espetada
- **O Jango** (Funchal)
- **O Polar** (Santo António, locals' choice)
- Any churrasqueira with wood fire visible

### Best View
- **Reid's Palace terrace** (afternoon tea or dinner)
- **Cachalote** (Porto Moniz) — above pools
- **Cliff Bay** (Funchal) — ocean panorama

### Best for Groups
- **O Jango** — communal espetada experience
- **Restaurante Combatentes** — big portions, local
- Any churrasqueira — shared meat platters

## Finding Hidden Gems

### How to Spot Good Local Places
- Handwritten menu or chalkboard specials
- Full of Madeirans at lunch
- One or two blocks off tourist strips
- No English menu (but staff will translate)
- Old decor, zero pretense

### Red Flags
- Photos of food in window
- "Traditional" + "Tourist menu"
- Someone trying to pull you in
- Prices not displayed
- Empty at local meal times

## Vegetarian/Vegan Options

Limited but improving:

| Name | Location | Type |
|------|----------|------|
| **Dona Amélia** | Funchal | Vegetarian options |
| **Terra** | Funchal | Health-focused |
| **Hotel restaurants** | Various | More accommodating |

Most restaurants can make:
- Vegetable soup (verify stock)
- Salads
- Grilled vegetables
- Egg dishes
- Pasta (if available)

Always ask: "Tem algo sem carne nem peixe?" (Do you have something without meat or fish?)
