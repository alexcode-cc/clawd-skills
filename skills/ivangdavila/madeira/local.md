# Madeira Local Services

## Banking

### Opening an Account

**Requirements**:
- Passport
- NIF (tax number)
- Proof of address
- Proof of income (some banks)

**Main Banks**:

| Bank | Type | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| ActivoBank | Online-first | Easiest for foreigners, free |
| Millennium BCP | Traditional | Largest, good Funchal coverage |
| CGD | State bank | Conservative, reliable |
| Novo Banco | Traditional | Branches available |
| Santander | International | Links to other Santander accounts |

**Process**:
1. Get NIF first
2. Visit bank branch with documents
3. Account usually open same day or within week
4. Card arrives by mail (1-2 weeks)

### Digital Banking Alternatives

| Service | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Wise | Multi-currency, transfers, card |
| Revolut | Daily spending, good rates |
| N26 | EU bank, easy to open |

**Reality**: Use Wise/Revolut for daily spending, Portuguese bank for direct debits and local needs.

### ATMs
- Multibanco network (MB) everywhere
- Free withdrawals from Portuguese cards
- Foreign cards: check fees
- €200-400 typical withdrawal limit

## Post & Mail

### CTT (Portuguese Post)

| Service | Time | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| To mainland Portugal | 2-3 days | Reliable |
| To EU | 5-7 days | Generally reliable |
| To USA/international | 7-14 days | Varies |

### Addresses
Portuguese addresses format:
```
Name
Street, Number
Postal Code Town
Portugal
```

Example:
```
João Silva
Rua Santa Maria 45, 2º Esq
9000-123 Funchal
Portugal
```

### Package Delivery
- CTT delivers most
- DHL, FedEx, UPS available (expensive)
- Amazon: Works but shipping costs higher
- Delivery times: Add 2-5 days vs. mainland

- Buy/exchange at supermarkets, gas stations

- Lasts 1-3 months depending on use

## Government Services

### Key Offices

| Office | Purpose | Location |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Finanças | Tax matters, NIF | Av. Zarco, Funchal |
| Câmara Municipal | Local registration, permits | Praça do Município |
| AIMA (ex-SEF) | Immigration | By appointment |
| Loja do Cidadão | One-stop for many services | Various |

### Getting NIF
- Go to Finanças
- Bring: Passport, address proof (even hotel receipt)
- Usually same-day
- Essential for everything (banking, rental, etc.)

## Practical Tips

### Business Hours

| Type | Typical Hours |
|------|---------------|
| Shops | 9:00-13:00, 15:00-19:00 |
| Malls | 10:00-22:00 |
| Banks | 8:30-15:00 (Mon-Fri) |
| Government | 9:00-16:00 (Mon-Fri) |
| Supermarkets | 8:30-21:00 |

**Sunday**: Many shops closed; supermarkets close early afternoon.
**Lunch break**: Traditional shops may close 13:00-15:00.

### Tipping Culture
- Not obligatory but appreciated
- 5-10% at restaurants for good service
- Round up for taxis, cafés
- No tip expected but never refused

### Greetings
- "Bom dia" (morning), "Boa tarde" (afternoon), "Boa noite" (evening)
- Shake hands (business/formal)
- Kiss on both cheeks (social, after knowing someone)
- "Obrigado" (thank you, male), "Obrigada" (female)
