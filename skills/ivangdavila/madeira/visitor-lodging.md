# Where to Stay in Madeira

## Quick Decision Guide

| Profile | Stay In | Budget/Night |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| First timer | Funchal center or old town | €80-150 |
| Luxury seeker | Belmond Reid's or Savoy | €300-600 |
| Digital nomad | Ponta do Sol or Funchal center | €50-100 (monthly better) |
| Hiker base | Funchal (good transport) or north (immersion) | €60-120 |
| Beach lover | Caniço or Machico | €60-100 |
| Budget traveler | Funchal hostels or airbnbs outside center | €20-60 |
| Families | Hotel zone (pools) or apartment | €100-180 |

## Funchal (Best for Most Visitors)

**Best for**: Atmosphere, dining, nightlife, walkability

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Best restaurants at doorstep | Can be noisy weekends |
| Painted doors, authentic vibe | Some areas touristy |
| Near market, cable car | Parking difficult |

| Name | Type | Price | Notes |
|------|------|-------|-------|

| Castanheiro | Boutique | €150-250 | Historic building, central |
| Santa Maria Hostel | Hostel | €25-40 | Social vibe, dorms + private |

| Good for families | Dining options limited |

## Accommodation Types

### Hotels
- **5-star**: €200-500/night, expect exceptional service
- **4-star**: €100-200/night, good facilities, pools
- **3-star**: €60-100/night, basic but clean

### Quintas (Manor Houses)
Traditional estates converted to hotels. Often include gardens, historic architecture, personalized service. €100-250/night.

Notable: Quinta da Casa Branca, Quinta Jardins do Lago

### Aparthotels
Best for longer stays or self-catering. Kitchen facilities, more space. €60-120/night.

Good for: Families, budget-conscious, long stays.

### Airbnb/VRBO
Wide range from €40-200/night. Tips:
- Check wifi speed (ask host)
- Verify parking situation
- Read recent reviews (some listings outdated)
- Funchal center = walkable, outside = need car

### Hostels
Only a few in Funchal:
- Santa Maria Hostel (best rated)
- Funchal Design Hostel
Expect €20-40 for dorms, €50-70 for private rooms.

## Seasonal Pricing

| Season | Dates | Price Multiplier |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| Low | Nov-Feb (excl. holidays) | 0.7-0.9x |
| Shoulder | Mar-May, Oct | 1.0x |
| High | Jun-Sep | 1.2-1.5x |
| Peak | Christmas, New Year, Easter | 1.5-2.0x |

**Book ahead**: Peak season books out 2-3 months ahead for good properties.

## Monthly Rentals (Long-Stay)

For digital nomads and extended stays:

| Area | Monthly Range | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Funchal center | €800-1,200 | Best location, competitive |
| Funchal outskirts | €600-900 | Car helpful |
| Ponta do Sol | €700-1,000 | Nomad community |
| Caniço | €600-850 | Quieter, beach access |
| Santa Cruz/Machico | €500-750 | Most affordable, local vibe |

**Finding monthly rentals:**
- Facebook groups: "Madeira Rentals", "Digital Nomads Madeira"
- Idealista.pt (Portuguese site, some English)
- Airbnb (filter for monthly, negotiate)
- Walk around areas — many "Aluga-se" signs not online

**Tips:**
- Negotiate directly for 10-20% off Airbnb prices
- Utilities usually included for short term, not always for 6+ months
- Check internet speed before committing
