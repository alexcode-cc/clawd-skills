### 4. Interview Scorecard System
Rate candidates 1-5 across these dimensions:
- **Technical competency** (weight: 30%) â€” can they do the work?
- **Problem-solving approach** (20%) â€” how do they think?
- **Communication clarity** (15%) â€” can they explain their thinking?
- **Culture contribution** (15%) â€” what do they ADD to the team?
- **Growth trajectory** (10%) â€” where are they heading?
- **Role-specific criteria** (10%) â€” domain expertise, tools, certifications

**Scoring**: 4.0+ = Strong Hire, 3.5-3.9 = Hire, 3.0-3.4 = Borderline (needs discussion), <3.0 = No Hire

### 5. Offer Engineering
```
Base salary: Market 50th percentile (conservative) or 65th (competitive)
Equity/options: 0.01-0.5% for IC, 0.5-2% for directors, 1-5% for VP+
Signing bonus: 10-20% of base for competitive situations
Benefits value: Add 25-35% to base for total comp messaging

Counter-offer response framework:
- Match within 10% â†’ counter immediately
- 10-20% gap â†’ add equity/signing bonus, don't inflate base
- >20% gap â†’ let them go (they'll leave in 6 months anyway)
```

### 6. Diversity & Inclusion Hiring
- Blind resume screening (remove names, schools, photos)
- Structured interviews only (same questions, same order, same rubric)
- Diverse interview panels (minimum 2 different backgrounds)
- Source from underrepresented communities (30%+ pipeline target)
- Track conversion rates by demographic at each stage

Track monthly:

### 10. Common Hiring Mistakes
1. **Hiring for culture "fit" instead of culture "add"** â€” fit = homogeneity, add = diversity of thought
2. **No structured interviews** â€” unstructured interviews predict performance at 14% vs 51% structured
3. **Ghosting candidates** â€” 72% share bad experiences, damages brand for years
4. **Optimizing for speed over quality** â€” a bad hire costs 30-50% of annual salary
5. **Ignoring internal candidates** â€” external hires take 3 years to match internal promotion performance
6. **Comp anchored to current salary** â€” pay for the role, not the person's history
7. **No hiring plan** â€” reactive hiring costs 2-3x proactive pipeline building
